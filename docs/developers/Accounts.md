## Hospital

### Who use this account? 

**one hospital's computer** login and asks for donations and follow can/can't.

### How to create account? 

Creating account is from `/signup-hospital` it's straighforward and easy form **but very strict so admins can easily filter scams.**

When the form is filled and Create Account button clicked, a message with these info are sent to admins NOT registerd in the platfrom directly, must be approved by admins to get registerd OTHERWISE the admin can refuse it if the documents are missing or the hospital is not honest etc..

#### Signup Form

1. hospital name
2. country
3. city
4. address
5. legal documents (verified by the government)
6. date of establishment
7. logo (image)
8. website (or facebook profile page, x, instagram etc.. **must has followers, active or valid domain e.g. '.com' or '.org' etc..)
9. phone number 
10. Email
11. Password
12. number of workers (doctors + workers)
13. number of last month's treated people (using documents not input number or est.)
14. 4 photos to upload (1: manager photo, 2: street photo, 3: entrance photo, hospital front  NO PATIENTS!)
15. Location on Google Maps

**Questions**

- why you want to make account?
- did you try alternatives before using the platform?
- how do you treat people in need?
- if X doesn't have money to pay the bill/medicine will you treat him for free until he pays or you will refuse politely until he pays?

**Checkboxs**

- I Swear to be 100% honest, treat people in need for free and ask for donations with true verified numbers & bills  
- I agree when caught lying the platform has the full right to involve the police and international courts against me
- I accpet terms and polices by CareBridge
